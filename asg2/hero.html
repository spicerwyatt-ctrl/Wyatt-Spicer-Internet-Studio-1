Kevin Smith

Kevin Smith was born on August 20th, 1970 in Red Bank, New Jersey. Kevin Smith is multitalented director, writer, actor and producer. He is known for numerous low budget buddy comedies.

Why Kevin Smith is an Inspiration and Hero

Kevin Smith has been working in the film industry for more than 30 years. In that time he has shown that he is not only a great filmmaker, but also a great friend due to the fact that he has consistently worked with many of his friends over the course of multiple decade.
