Blink 182

Blink 182 is a rock band from California, founded in 1992 by Tom Delonge, Mark Hoppus, and Scott Raynor. Scott Raynor was eventually replaced by Travis Barker. Blink 182 has released numerous studio albums and have had numerous world tours. Though its sound has diversified throughout their career, its musical style, described as pop-punk, blends catchy pop melodies with fast-paced punk rock. Its lyrics primarily focus on relationships, adolescent frustration, and maturity, or lack thereof. The group emerged from a suburban, Southern California skate punk scene and first gained notoriety for high-energy live shows and irreverent humor" (From Wiki).

Why Blink 182 is my FAVORITE band:

Ever since I was a child I have been obsessed with Blink 182 and their high energy, comedic tones. Part of the reason for this obsession was due to the fact that I was raised as a drummer, and as a drummer was immediately focused on trying to mimic anything that the drummer, Travis Barker, plays. It got to a point where I was in pop punk cover band in middle school. Aside from Taylor Swift, Blink 182 is the band I have seen the most live. While most of my friends have grown out of their Blink 182 obsessions, I have not.
